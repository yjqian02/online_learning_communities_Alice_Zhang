Computer Science Learning Communities within Homework Help

The community I am exploring is the #computer-science channel within the Homework Help Discord server. This server is very popular among students with 20k members active usually and around 165k members total. Members here usually interact asynchronously via text and at most hop on voice/video calls. There are assigned roles for people who answer questions frequently, but everything is done on a volunteer basis and moderators regularly enforce academic integrity and other community guidelines. Other than the subject content, a lot of what people learn how to do in this server is formulate a question to ask asynchronously using text + images + other (like code).
